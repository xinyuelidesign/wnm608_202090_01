  2. Put a small amount of oil in the pan (as long as it spreads evenly on the bottom of the pan), turn on the heat.
  3. Place the dumplings in the pot (do not need to defrost, put them directly).
  4. Fry on medium heat for a minute or two, until you hear a sizzling sound, the bottom of the dumplings starts to turn golden brown.
  5. At this time, pour half a bowl of water, the amount of water is about one-third of the dumplings.Cover the lid and cook on medium heat for about five minutes.
  6. Taking advantage of this gap, use flour and water to make a thin flour and water at a ratio of about 1:10. When there is a sizzling noise in the pot, it is getting louder and louder. At this time, the water in the pot is almost dry. Open the lid and pour the diluted flour and water evenly into the pot (if you want to sprinkle sesame and green onion, sprinkle it at this time Into), turn to low heat and fry until the water in the pan is dry, the flour and water will form a crisp film on the bottom of the pan, then turn off the fire.
  7. Done!
